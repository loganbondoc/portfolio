Atlassian X Suede Designathon

The Designathon was a competition hosted by both Atlassian and the SUEDE university club, where groups of 3 - 4 were tasked with designing solutions for a proposed issue in the span of 3 days. For this competition, the group and myself designed an application "Kamoy" that would streamline both communication and collaboration within the workplace. To create this, we went through the process of conducting user research through interviews and user personas then creating wireframes in Figma to test their useability considerations. The final product was a high fidelity prototype of the application in action.

Smart Gate

Through investigating the Sydney Train System, our team found issues pertaining to comfortability, efficiency, safety and navigation. To combat this we designed the "Smart Gate", and conducted user testing upon its usability and effectiveness within the real world.
